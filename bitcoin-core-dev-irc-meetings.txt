Bitcoin Core Dev IRC Meetings
-----------------------------

*General* meeting every Thursday 1900 UTC on #bitcoin-core-dev

  General meeting logs:
  http://www.erisian.com.au/meetbot/bitcoin-core-dev/

  To link to a specific line in the log history, append #l-<line-number>
  http://www.erisian.com.au/bitcoin-core-dev/log-2019-04-16.html#l-129

  Past and future topics are managed by Steve Lee (moneyball on IRC) in this gist:
  https://gist.github.com/moneyball/071d608fdae217c2a6d7c35955881d8a

  To propose a meeting topic, post a message to moneyball on #bitcoin-core-dev:
  moneyball: #proposedmeetingtopic <topic>

-----

*Wallet* meeting every other Friday 1900 UTC on #bitcoin-core-dev since 29/10/18

  Wallet meeting logs (same link as for the General meetings):
  http://www.erisian.com.au/meetbot/bitcoin-core-dev/

-----

*PR Review Club* meeting every Wednesday 1700 UTC on #bitcoin-core-pr-reviews

  Website: https://bitcoincore.reviews

  PR Review Club meeting logs are available on the website by clicking on the
  past meeting links.

  To suggest a PR or offer to host a meeting, leave a comment at:

  https://github.com/bitcoin-core-review-club/bitcoin-core-review-club.github.io/issues/14

-----

Commands to host a #bitcoin-core-dev meeting:

  #startmeeting
  #endmeeting
  #action
  #agreed
  #help
  #info
  #idea
  #link
  #topic

  The main commands used are #startmeeting, #topic, #action, and #endmeeting.

-----

We start Bitcoin Core IRC meetings with a 'hi' so it's clear who is present.
Feel free to say hi!

Note that saying "hi" or posting on the channel at any time during a
#bitcoin-core-dev meeting adds you to archived meeting log and minutes.
